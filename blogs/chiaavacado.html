Slim Down & Soothe: Chia Avocado Smoothie

Why This Smoothie?

Discover a smoothie that satisfies your taste buds while reducing inflammation and supporting weight loss. This nutrient-packed recipe combines chia seeds, avocado, and honeydew melon for a perfect start to your day.

  • Chia Seeds: Rich in antioxidants, protein, and omega-3 fatty acids to reduce inflammation and boost metabolism.
  • Avocado: Provides healthy fats and fiber to keep you full and reduce inflammation.
  • Cucumber & Honeydew Melon: Low-calorie, hydrating, and anti-inflammatory.
  • Mint: Refreshes and aids digestion.
  • Vanilla Protein Powder: Optional but supports muscle recovery and energy.

Recipe Details

Ingredient Quantity (g)
Unsweetened almond milk 375 ml (~375 g)
Avocado 100 g
Chopped cucumber 150 g
Honeydew melon 150 g
Fresh mint 10 g
Chia seeds 28 g (2 tbsp)
Vanilla protein powder (optional) 30 g (1 scoop)
Ice 120 g

Preparation Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into your favorite glass or jar and enjoy!

Why It Works: This smoothie offers a perfect blend of protein, fiber, and healthy fats to keep you full and energized while combating inflammation. Its low-glycemic ingredients prevent blood sugar spikes, making it a nutrient-dense and satisfying choice.
